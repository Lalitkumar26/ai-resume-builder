Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ResumeAI.

Is ResumeAI free to use?
Yes! ResumeAI is completely free to use. You can create, edit, and download your resume without any cost. We believe everyone deserves access to professional resume-building tools.
How does the AI feature work?
Our AI uses advanced language models (powered by Google Gemini) to analyze your job title, experience, and skills to generate professional, tailored content for your resume. It can write summaries, enhance job descriptions, and suggest relevant skills based on your career field.
Can I download my resume as a PDF?
Absolutely! Once you're happy with your resume, simply click the "Download PDF" button in the builder page. Your resume will be instantly converted to a high-quality PDF file that you can send directly to employers.
Are the resume templates ATS-friendly?
Yes! All our templates, especially the Classic Professional template, are designed to be ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly. They use clean formatting, proper headings, and standard fonts to ensure your resume passes through ATS systems successfully.
Is my data secure?
Your privacy and security are our top priority. All data is stored securely using Firebase with industry-standard encryption. Your resume data is private and only accessible by you. We never share your personal information with third parties.
Can I switch templates after building my resume?
Yes! You can switch between our templates at any time in the builder page without losing any of your data. Simply click on the template buttons in the preview panel and your content will automatically reformat to the new template.
